---
title: On keeping a notebook
date: 2026-04-20
description: Why half-formed thoughts deserve a home before they turn into anything else.
tags: [life]
---

I keep a notebook mostly because I forget things. Not the important things — those tend to announce themselves — but the small observations that don't fit anywhere else. A phrase overheard on a bus. A half-formed idea on the walk home. A sentence from a book I'll never quote but don't want to let go.

Most of them don't survive the week. That's fine. The notebook isn't for the ideas that survive; it's for the ones that might. Writing them down is a way of saying *maybe this*.

The rest is just editing.

date: 2026-04-20

Writing in public is useful even when no one reads it — it forces me to be honest about what I actually understand versus what I'm waving at.

## Topics I'll probably write about

- Tech notes from whatever I'm building
- Book takeaways and reading notes
- Occasional life stuff

## Topics I probably won't

- Industry hot takes
- Anything that needs me to pretend to be certain
